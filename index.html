I am a Research Intern working in collaboration with Dianbo Liu and Anirudh Goyal working on leveraging LLMs and VLMs to equip Embodied AI Agents with Human Inspired Cognitive Intelligence

Previously, I was a Research Assistant advised by Madhav Krishna, Mohan Sridharan and Krishna Murthy Jatavallabula at IIIT-Hyderabad. Here I worked on commonsense based object navigation in household robots. I obtained my bachelors in B.E Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering from BITS Pilani, Pilani in 2022. My bachelor's thesis was based on developing Honey Bee Vision inspired Obstacle Avoidance Algorithms under the guidance of Sridhar Ravi

My research interests lie at the intersection of Cognitive Science, CommonSense Reasoning and Robotics. Prior to shifting to Human Cognition Inspired Robotics, in my undergrad I was extremely passionate about Insect Behavior Inspired Intelligence and Collective Intelligence.

Embodied Physical CommonSense Affordance
Ayush A, Raghav P, Anirudh G, Dianbo Liu
[In Submission]|arxiv|code

Demystifying the decision making process behind choosing an object for task completion, we develop a 3 step architecture and curate datasets to power future research in this domain. Further, we evaluate various LLM baselines and report the findings.


Sequence Agnostic MultiON
Nandiraju Gireesh*, Ayush A*, Ahana Datta* ... Madhav Krishna
ICRA 2023 |arxiv|video|blog

You are already in a kitchen, and tasked to find a fridge. Would you search for it in the current area or other places in house? We train a RL policy based off semantic relationship between static objects to generate efficient long term goals to enable quick retreival of a list of objects.


CLIPGraphs: Multimodal Graph Networks to Infer Object-Room Affinities
Ayush A*, Raghav Arora*, ... Madhav Krishna
RO-MAN 2023 |arxiv|code|page

Building upon the idea that we humans have highly developed Object-Utility and Room-Utility relationships, we develop a Graph Neural Network by processing Human Preference datasets and Foundation Model Features to generate human commonsense aligned Object-Room Affinities for dynamic objects. The generated Latent Embeddings would be useful for downstream embodied AI tasks.
