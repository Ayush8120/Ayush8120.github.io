About Me

I am a pre-final year undergraduate student at the Computer Science Department at BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. I am deeply interested in robotics, specifically in the area of motion planning. I am also exploring new areas related to deep learning, and am actively exploring reinforcement learning.

I am currently working with Prof. Avinash Gautam on a multi-robot path planning project, exploiting the formation of clusters from frontiers for optimal coverage of the environment. I am also developing my computer vision skills while working on anamoly detection in videos. This project is being done under the supervision of Prof. Navneet Goyal. I spent my last summer with the A.R.M.S. Lab at IIT Bombay headed by Prof. Arpita Sinha, exploring the navigation stack for a tractor trailer robotic system. I have previously worked on a ML based person state classification project at CEERI Chennai under the mentorship of Dr Madan Kumar Lakshmanan.

I am always looking forward to new opportunities!
